Below are selected real-world VFD repair, panel service, and power quality projects executed by Erica Engineering Works. These case studies demonstrate our chip-level repair expertise and practical industrial solutions.

Case Study 01 – 15 kW VFD Repair (Textile Dyeing Unit)

Industry: Textile Dyeing

VFD Rating: 15 kW

Application: Dyeing machine circulation pump

Brand: Multi-brand AC Drive

Customer Issue:

  • VFD tripping on overcurrent
  • Drive not powering ON intermittently

Root Cause Analysis:
IGBT short circuit due to load imbalance and damaged gate driver IC.

Solution Implemented:

  • Chip-level IGBT replacement
  • Gate driver circuit repair
  • DC bus and power stage testing
  • Full-load testing before dispatch

Result:
VFD restored to stable operation with reduced downtime and improved reliability.

Case Study 02 – 30 kW VFD Communication Failure

Industry: Plastic Processing

VFD Rating: 30 kW

Application: Extruder motor control

Problem Statement:

  • No communication between PLC and VFD
  • Frequent fault alarms during operation

Technical Diagnosis:
Control PCB failure and damaged opto-isolators in communication circuit.

Corrective Action:

  • Control card repair at component level
  • Opto-isolator replacement
  • Firmware integrity check
  • PLC communication testing

Outcome:
Stable PLC communication achieved and production downtime eliminated.
